Fogg is a body spray and deodorant brand owned by Vini Cosmetics, an Indian company based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. The brand was launched in 2011 by Darshan Patel, the founder and managing director of Vini Cosmetics. The name Fogg is an acronym for "Friend of Good Guys/Girls".
